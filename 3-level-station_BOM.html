Bill of Materials

Download this as a CSV file

Tools

  • 1 #1 pozidrive screwdriver
  • 1 2.5mm Ball-end Allen key
  • 1 Crimper
  • 1 Laser cutting machine
  • 1 M3 tap
  • 1 Needle-nose plier
  • 1 Pi Camera lens tool - This should come with the Raspberry Pi Camera Module. If it is missing, you can 3D print a workaround lens remover.
  • 1 Precision wire cutter
  • 1 RepRap-style printer
  • 1 Soldering iron
  • 1 Tap wrench
  • 1 Utility knife - Not a scalpel!

Consumables

  • 1 drop of light oil
  • a little Lubricant

Electronic Components

  • 1 2 pin Du Pont connector female housing
  • 1 30x30x10mm Heat sink
  • 1 Double-sided self adhesive tape
  • 1 Double-sided thermal tape
  • 1 High-power star LED
  • 2 Male Crimp Pin
  • 1 Pi camera ribbon cable
  • 1 Pi Hat
  • 1 Raspberry Pi
  • 1 Raspberry Pi Camera Module v2
  • 1 Raspberry Pi Power Supply
  • 1 Strobe Cable
  • 1 Strobe Module
  • 1 Strobe Power Supply
  • 1 Voltage regulator

Materials

  • 1 52x18x0.5cm Acrylic sheet
  • 50 g of Black PLA filament
  • 205 g of PLA filament - Of any colour you want

Mechanical Components

  • 7 Heat-set insert
  • 1 M3 brass nut
  • 13 M3 nut
  • 2 M3 washers
  • 11 M3x10mm cap head screw
  • 8 M3x15mm cap head screw
  • 1 M3x20mm cap head screw
  • 4 M3x25mm cap head screw
  • 1 M3x25mm hex head screw
  • 2 M3x6mm cap head screw
  • 4 No 2 6.5mm self tapping screw
  • 1 O-ring

Optical Components

  • 1 8.7° 20 mm Circular Beam Optic
